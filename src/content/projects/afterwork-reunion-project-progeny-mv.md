<div class="lang-zh">

這部 MV 的核心創意圍繞著音樂與視覺的跨世代演進。全片將「8-bit 電子音效」到「現代全音域高頻與低頻震盪」的聽覺轉變，精準轉譯為「2D 點陣」與「3D 空間」的視覺切換，讓影像隨著音軌的擴張而同步進化。

為了呈現其中的 2D 動畫橋段，製作上特別從零學習遊戲製作軟體，並設計出一套完整的復古遊戲。片中所有的 2D 畫面並非傳統分鏡合成，而是直接在遊戲運作時即時錄製捕捉，藉此還原最真實的遊戲視角。

在生成式 AI 盛行的當下，這部作品選擇回歸手作。2D 角色的所有動態與逐幀細節，全數堅持在 Photoshop 中手工繪製完成，並未投入任何 AI 輔助。同時，單曲封面也延續相同的工藝精神，同樣採用手工繪製，讓視覺風格從影像到靜態封面保持高度統一。

此外，為了回應歌手對經典遊戲《洛克人》（Mega Man）的風格要求，片中從關卡美學、UI 介面到像素動態，都深度融合了該系列的獨特元素。最核心的巧思在於，遊戲關卡中登場的每隻怪物，都象徵著當代的各類音樂現象：速食音樂被化身為「漢堡怪」；「Emo 人」代表了 Emo 音樂；「無聊猿」（Bored Ape）暗喻粗製濫造的懶人音樂；「重影機器人」諷刺許多音樂聽起來千篇一律的複製現象；最後更將流行音樂象徵化為「葛萊美獎盃怪物」。

主角一路擊敗這些代表現代音樂流派與現象的怪物，不僅重現了橫向捲軸遊戲的經典趣味，更將 2D 復古情懷與現代 3D 視覺流暢結合，打造出一場橫跨聽覺與視覺維度的音樂影像實驗。

<div class="lang-en">

This MV centres on the cross-generational evolution of music and visuals. The film translates the sonic shift from 8-bit electronic sounds to modern full-spectrum frequencies into a visual switch between 2D pixel art and 3D space — the image evolving in sync with the expanding soundtrack.

To realise the 2D animation sequences, the production team learned a game development engine from scratch and built a complete retro game. All 2D footage was captured as live recordings during actual gameplay rather than composited from storyboards, preserving the most authentic game perspective possible.

In an era of generative AI, this work chose to go handmade. Every frame of 2D character animation was drawn manually in Photoshop with no AI assistance whatsoever. The single cover art follows the same craft philosophy — hand-drawn to maintain complete visual consistency from motion to static image.

At the artist's request to reference classic game Mega Man, the production embedded the series' distinctive elements throughout — level aesthetics, UI design, and pixel movement. The central creative conceit: each monster in the game levels symbolises a contemporary music phenomenon. Fast-food music becomes the Burger Monster; the Emo character represents emo music; the Bored Ape alludes to lazy, low-effort releases; the Shadow Robot satirises the endless sonic copies flooding modern music; and the Grammy Trophy Monster stands in for mainstream pop.

As the protagonist defeats each monster, the film recreates the classic pleasure of side-scrolling games while blending 2D retro nostalgia with modern 3D visuals — a musical and visual experiment spanning both auditory and visual dimensions.
