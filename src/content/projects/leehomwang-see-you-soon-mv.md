<div class="lang-zh">

在這支王力宏的 MV 中，我們負責了兩個主要的後製工作。

其一是影片中大量氣球的視覺特效製作。氣球在實際拍攝中極難控制——它會受風向影響，動態難以預測，更不可能精準引導它的移動軌跡。因此，影片中的氣球大部分以 3D 方式製作，取代真實拍攝，讓畫面中的氣球運動能夠完全按照創作意圖呈現。

其二是大量的場景清理工作。由於拍攝現場的時代背景設定，畫面中出現了多棟不符合場景時間軸的現代高樓大廈。後製過程中，這些建築需要逐幀從背景中移除，以確保畫面的視覺一致性與敘事真實感。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

Our involvement in this Leehom Wang music video centred on two areas of post-production work.

The first was the balloon sequences throughout the film. Balloons are notoriously difficult to shoot practically — they respond unpredictably to air movement, and directing their motion on set is effectively impossible. For this reason, the majority of the balloons visible in the video were created in 3D, giving the production full control over where they move and how they behave within each shot.

The second was extensive scene cleanup. The shoot took place against a backdrop that included several modern high-rise buildings — structures that were inconsistent with the visual world the MV was trying to establish. A significant portion of the post work involved removing these buildings frame by frame, restoring the integrity of the scene's setting.

</div>
