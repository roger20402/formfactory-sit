<div class="lang-zh">

這支 MV 從接案到交付，整個製作週期不到兩週。製作範疇主要集中在片頭與片尾的視覺特效製作，以及數個過場轉場——包含鏡頭穿越傳送門的縮放效果，以及鉚釘拋擲的動態特效。
在緊迫的時間軸下維持去背與合成的精確度，尤其是大量的綠幕去背與逐幀 Rotoscoping 遮罩工作，是這個專案在技術執行上最耗時的部分。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

This MV was delivered in under two weeks from brief to final output. The work focused primarily on the intro and outro sequences, along with several transition effects — including camera zooms through portals and the rivet-throwing sequences that punctuate the edit.
The most time-consuming aspect of the production was the green screen removal and frame-by-frame rotoscoping required to integrate the live-action footage cleanly within that compressed timeline.
</div>
