<div class="lang-zh">

這支動畫是為 ASUS 華碩的 Business Solution 服務所製作。ASUS Business Solution 是 ASUS 針對企業客戶所提供的一套完整解決方案，而這支影片的任務，是以 3D 動畫作為視覺輔助，幫助消費者理解這項服務的運作方式與內容。

在這個專案中，我們負責了從美術方向、敘事結構到各場景構圖的全面藝術指導。如何將一項抽象的企業服務轉化為清晰、直觀的視覺語言，是這支影片在創作上的核心課題。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

This animation was produced for ASUS Business Solution, a service offering from ASUS aimed at enterprise clients. Rather than a product animation, the brief was to create a visual aid — a 3D piece that helps viewers understand what the service is, how it works, and what it provides, making something inherently abstract tangible and clear.

The scope included full creative direction: art direction, narrative structure, and scene composition were all developed from the ground up. Translating a service into coherent visual storytelling, without a physical product to anchor it, was the central creative challenge of the project.
