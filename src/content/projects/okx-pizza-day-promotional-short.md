<div class="lang-zh">

這是為 OKX 的 Pizza Day 活動所製作的宣傳短片，活動地點位於台北市政府捷運站。整支影片走輕鬆幽默的路線，以視覺特效打造一個出乎意料的場景：一個巨大的披薩從天而降，砸向活動舉辦地點——披薩上的海苔圖案正是 OKX 的品牌標誌。緊接著，肉丸滾落街道，被一輛路過的機車撞上。

整體風格俏皮、搶眼，目的是在 Instagram 上吸引目光，同時為線下活動製造話題與期待感。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

This promotional short was produced for OKX's Pizza Day event, held at Taipei City Hall MRT station. The concept is playful and deliberately absurd: a giant pizza crashes down from the sky onto the event venue, its seaweed topping shaped in the OKX logo. Meatballs roll out across the road and get hit by a passing motorcycle.

The piece was designed to stop people mid-scroll on Instagram — eye-catching, funny, and just strange enough to make you watch it twice. A lighter side of VFX production, but no less technically involved for it.

</div>
