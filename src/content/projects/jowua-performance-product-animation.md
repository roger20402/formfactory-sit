<div class="lang-zh">

Jowua 是一個專為 Tesla 車主開發配件產品的品牌。這支動畫是為他們最新的電動滑板車產品線所製作，目的是以 3D 動畫清楚示範產品的拆解與組裝流程。

這類教學性質的動畫在製作上有其特殊的挑戰：每一個步驟的時間節奏都必須經過仔細拿捏，既要讓觀看者有足夠的時間理解每個動作，又不能讓整體影片拖沓到讓人失去耐心看完。清晰度與流暢度之間的平衡，是這支動畫的核心課題。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

Jowua is a brand known for developing accessories for Tesla owners. This animation was produced for their newest electric scooter product line, with a brief focused on demonstrating the product's disassembly and assembly process through 3D animation.

Instructional animation of this kind presents a particular pacing challenge: each step needs to be held long enough for the viewer to absorb the action, while the overall edit has to move quickly enough to hold attention through to the end. Too slow and it becomes a chore to watch; too fast and the instructions stop making sense. Finding that balance — clarity without losing the viewer — was the central problem the animation had to solve.

</div>
