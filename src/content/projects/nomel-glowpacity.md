<div class="lang-zh">

這次放飛設計為 Nomel Glowpacity 系列（包含 melt、oil 與 mist 三款產品）所創作的 3D 產品動畫。視覺風格核心在於營造獨特的對比感，將精緻的保養品線，置於一個充滿結構感、金屬感且具未來主義色彩的實驗室環境中。

整個製作流程從初期概念發展至最終畫面，工作重心集中在輸送帶系統的設計與構成上。這套機械系統以多幅前期草圖為基礎，逐步發展成一個在畫面中具有強烈視覺存在感的工業裝置——其開場的配置方式、鏡頭構圖，以及各個機械元件之間的空間關係，是整個專案中投入最多設計思考的部分，也是我們最滿意的成果。

整體金屬質感的呈現是這支影片另一個核心成就。冷調、精密、帶有未來感的工廠氛圍，為 Glowpacity 系列建立了一個強烈的視覺背景，讓產品在其中既顯得珍貴，又與整體美學高度契合。

</div>

<div class="lang-en">

This animation was produced for Nomel, a Taiwanese skincare brand, covering their Glowpacity line across three product formats — melt, oil, and mist. The visual direction centres on a deliberate contrast: delicate skincare products set within a structured, metallic, futurist laboratory environment.

The core of the production was the design and composition of the conveyor belt system that runs through the piece. Developed from early sketch concepts, the system grew into a mechanically detailed industrial apparatus with a strong visual presence — the opening configuration, the spatial arrangement of each component, and the way the overall composition frames the products were the aspects of the project that demanded the most design thinking, and that we're most satisfied with.

The execution of the factory's metallic aesthetic is what we consider the standout success of this project. The cold, precise, future-facing atmosphere of the environment gives the Glowpacity line a visual context that feels both elevated and entirely consistent — machinery and skincare occupying the same world convincingly.
